What we do

We are an aspiring nonprofit organization with the aim of providing free and high quality mentorship in science to diminish barriers in access to rigorous STEM education. We provide direct mentorship to students from marginalized backgrounds, and we currently offer a virtual summer research program in neuroscience.

Neuroscience

In the summer-long immersive experience, students will work with mentors on a research project.
