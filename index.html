Our Mission

Everyone deserves an opportunity
to pursue science.


Founded with a vision to destroy barriers to science, we provide research training to high school students across the US at no cost.

We hope to empower a diverse generation of scientists by providing a free and and accessible science program to all.

Revolutionizing Research Education

To overcome traditional limitations in resources in research mentorship, we pair students with mentors for project-based learning.

Our Team

Founded by a lover of scientific inquiry, our team is made of diverse students with a shared passion for science outreach and advocacy.

Ally Kim

Founding director, CEO

As the first in her immigrant family to pursue scientific research, Ally witnessed the hurdles in gaining access to research training. She founded Sci-MI with the goal of increasing access to training and empowering diverse scientists. Beyond Sci-MI, Ally works at the Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard to bridge the diversity gap in genomics research.
