Our Mission

Everyone deserves an opportunity
to pursue science.


Founded with a vision to destroy barriers to science and empower a diverse generation of scientists, we provide research training to high school students across the US at no cost.



Revolutionizing research education

To overcome traditional limitations in resources in research mentorship, we pair students with mentors for free project-based learning.
